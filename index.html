About Us

VilvusGames is a one man mobile game development studio. We are based in HÃ¤meenlinna Finland. VilvusGames the company was founded in July of 2020 by a high school graduate and our first game was realased soon after. I live by myself and work a part time job. I maintain VilvusGames on my spare time


In the year 2016, I started experimenting with Gamemaker Studio 1.8 and the android export module. Soon after this the Idea for VilvusGames was born. Our first two games were SimplyJump and BounceIt. They were half baked, merely playable game ideas, that I decided to publish on the Google Playstore. They hardly got any attention, but they got me hooked on game making.


Our first real game was Crate Crash Pilots. The development process took about 6 months and everyting except sound effects were done by me.
